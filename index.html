A non-profit technology company

Mission

Open Focus supports, fosters and promotes innovative use of open source technology solutions for humanity. We operate online and offline, at grassroots and at global level, to promote collaborative approaches based on open source models. Our flagship initiative is called High Tech Humanitarians(HTH). HTH facilitates access and unhindered innovation leveraging open source technology through an online repository and open digital labs.
